## Core Words

| German | Article/Plural | English | Example Sentence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Termin | der / die Termine | appointment | Ich habe einen Termin. |
| Adresse | die / die Adressen | address | Meine Adresse ist ... |
| Formular | das / die Formulare | form | Bitte füllen Sie das Formular aus. |

Noun rule: every noun must include gender and plural. If a vocabulary table does not use a separate `Article/Plural` column, put the full noun form directly in the German column, such as `der Termin, die Termine`.

For plural-only or mostly singular nouns, mark the pattern clearly:

- `die Schmerzen (Plural)`
- `das Wasser, meist Singular`

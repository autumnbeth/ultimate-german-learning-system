# Situation Roadmap

This document tracks real-life situations planned for the German Learning System.

The goal is not to organize content by textbook chapters.

The goal is to organize content around situations learners actually encounter in daily life.

Status values:

* Planned
* In Progress
* Complete

# Roadmap Philosophy

Situations are added because they represent meaningful real-life needs for adult learners.

The roadmap should stay simple, practical, and easy to update.
